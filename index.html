// Features

Built for the workbench, not the boardroom

~1ms Timestamp Precision

Captured at the Rust reader thread before OS scheduling can intervene, every frame carries a monotonic delta (ΔT) so you can spot timing violations at a glance.

HEX / DEC / ASCII Grid

Stop toggling between views — see all three encodings simultaneously, aligned to configurable bytes-per-row width. Virtual scroll handles 10,000+ entries without a stutter.

F1–F10 Macro Slots

Store up to 10 commands in HEX, ASCII, or DEC format and fire them with a single function key. Slots persist across sessions — your test sequences are ready the moment you reconnect.

Auto CSV Logging

Every received frame is written to disk automatically in CSV format with configurable column width. Open directly in Excel — no exporting, no copy-paste, no conversion.

Frame Detection AUTO / MANUAL

AUTO mode learns packet boundaries from inter-byte timing gaps. MANUAL mode locks to a fixed byte width. Switch mid-session without disconnecting or losing your log.

TX History & Quick Send

Every transmission is logged with a HH:MM:SS.mmm timestamp and full HEX dump. The send bar stays visible in the LOG tab — fire a command without leaving your log view.
